Welcome

Pro gaming or e-sports can be a really fun way for all kinds of different people and although it’s not the first thing that comes to mind when you think about extracurricular activities, that doesn’t mean it’s a bad choice. Pro-gaming encourages you to discuss with all kinds of people what they think the best way to achieve a goal is, whether it be beating the enemy team in a Players Vs Player game or getting the fastest lap in a racing game. Pro gaming can help you meet new people that have a shared interest with and can possibly become good friends with.
